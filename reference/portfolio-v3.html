About

I'm a frontend developer who gravitates toward the intersection of data, real-time systems, and visual design. I enjoy building things that make complex information feel immediate and tangible — whether that's live transit data rendered on a map or baseball stats surfaced through a chat bot.

My sweet spot is working with APIs, vanilla JavaScript, and D3.js to craft interfaces that feel alive. I'm drawn to projects where the data itself becomes the design — where structure and motion emerge directly from what's happening in the real world.

Outside of code, I'm into maker projects, following the Cubs season, and figuring out the best way to mount an LED matrix to a wall.
